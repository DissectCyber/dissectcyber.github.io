GNAT Cleaner Link
When you tap COPY…
Shows your apps list again, ready to share the clean link:
Tap Share …
This time don’t choose GNAT. Just choose the app you want to share the clean link to.
Opens the clean link in Safari for a web link, or the social media app the link was created for.
Tap Open …

A friend sends you a chat message with Twitter link full of trackers.

 

You long press the link, choose SHARE -> GNAT Cleaner.

 

Now you can OPEN the clean link.

 

When would I use OPEN?
Closes GNAT without doing anything. It’s like cancelling.
Tap Dismiss …
Let’s say you changed your mind and you don’t want to use the clean link right now. You just want to start over or do something different.
